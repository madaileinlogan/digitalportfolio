Projects and PBL (Project Based Learning) are both an important part of the Dayton Regional STEM School's culture. During projects, we bond with other students and grow in The 5 Qualities. PBL is a teaching style where students learn while doing the project. This is instead of learning all of the content before and then completing an assignment. When PBL is used, students can gain a deeper understanding of the content and learn from their mistakes, helping them to grow.
